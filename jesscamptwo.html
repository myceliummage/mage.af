Activities

At the house we'll cook together each evening, play music, sing, impro, dance, spoon, hug, make art, paint faces.

There will be a whiteboard where people can propose activities on post-its and schedule a time for it by placing the postit on a simplified calendar.

Suggestions so far inlcude:

  • A run through the city, ending in eating a pastel de nata
  • Tea tasting

The local area provides plenty of entertainment, so depending on the weather we can also go to museums, look at the river, hang in cafes and visit parks and miradouros (scenic viewpoints).
